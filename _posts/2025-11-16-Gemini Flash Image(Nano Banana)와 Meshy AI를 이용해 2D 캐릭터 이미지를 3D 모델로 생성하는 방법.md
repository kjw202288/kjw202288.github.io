1. <https://aistudio.google.com/models/gemini-2-5-flash-image>에 들어가 나노바나나를 선택한다

2. 프롬프트를 입력해 캐릭터를 원하는 구도로 맞춘뒤 Hyper-realistic figure, based on the provided drawing, identical pose, same outfit, ultra-detailed, 8K, cinematic lighting, high-quality painted plastic texture, hand-painted details, subtle weathering, realistic shading, studio lighting, natural shadows, soft reflections, very light gray studio backdrop, standing on floor, no base, no stand을 프롬프트로 입력하여 3D 피규어 형상을 얻는다

3. <meshy.ai>에 접속하여 3D 모델을 얻는다
